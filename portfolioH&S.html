Direct Support Professional

June 2018 - July 2020
Meaningful Day Services

This was a part-time career I took up during breaks between semesters, in which I would care for and assist various clients with developmental disabilities in daily scheduled programs with occasional trips to locations in the community, such as the local zoo or park. In preparation, I was also certified by the American Red Cross in Adult and Child CPR/First Aid (06/20/2018 with a validity period of 2 years), enabling me to administer and chart various medication treatments for clients. I assisted clients to lead a self-directed life and contribute to the community through scheduled activities, guided-personalized behavioral development plans, education development plans, and volunteer work.

Clinical Laboratory Technologist

December 2021 - December 2022
Joint employment under HHC and the Marion County Public Health Department.

Under general supervision and direction, performs waived, moderate, and high complexity clinical laboratory procedures as defined in the Clinical Laboratory Improvement Act of 1988. Supports the assessment of health, as one of the core functions of public health, by diagnosing and investigating health problems and health hazards in the community. Work experience at multiple sites and clinics including Eskenazi Hospital, Action Health Center, and the MCPHD Hasbrook Building.

Johnson Memorial Hospital Physician Internship

January 2020
Overseen by John Rhea, JMH Special Projects Coordinator

Observed and assisted medical practitioners in the Immediate Care Center, Anesthesia, and Internal Medicine departments throughout the hospital facility while shadowing present physicians (MD & DO), PAs, and other levels of primary care staff.
